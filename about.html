Purpose

This website is a fun and interactive gallery dedicated to the legendary Avengers. It showcases some of the most iconic Marvel heroes, complete with images and short bios.

The purpose of the site is to celebrate the Avengers, give fans a simple way to explore their favorite characters, and practice clean web design using HTML and CSS.

Whether you're Team Iron Man or Team Cap, there's something here for every Marvel fan. Enjoy exploring!
